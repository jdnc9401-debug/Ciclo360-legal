# Privacy Policy for Ciclo360

**Last updated: March 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the **Ciclo360** application (hereinafter, “the App”), developed by **Valynx** (hereinafter, “the Company”, “we”, or “our”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

---

## 🔹 1. Data Controller

**Valynx**  
**Country:** Spain  
**Contact email:** valynxsolutions@outlook.com

---

## 🔹 2. Purpose of the Application

Ciclo360 is an application designed to record and manage the menstrual cycle, allowing users to keep personal track of their reproductive health.

---

## 🔹 3. Data We Collect

### 🟣 3.1. User-entered data (Health data)

- Menstrual cycle information (dates, symptoms, notes, etc.)

📌 **Important:** This data is stored **only locally** on the user’s device.  
It is **not sent** to external servers or Firebase.

---

### 🟣 3.2. Automatically collected data (Usage and diagnostics)

The App uses Firebase services that may collect:

- App usage data  
- Device type  
- Operating system version  
- Advertising identifiers (for AdMob)  
- Technical information for diagnostics and performance  

This data **does not include menstrual cycle information**.

---

### 🟣 3.3. Device permissions

#### 📷 Camera
- Used only to scan a linking QR code.  
- No photos or videos are stored.

#### 🔔 Push notifications
- Used to send reminders and notifications related to the App’s functionality.

---

## 🔹 4. Legal Basis (GDPR – Europe)

Data processing is based on:

- User consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR)  
- Legitimate interest to improve the App (Art. 6.1.f GDPR)  
- Local processing of health data under the user’s exclusive control (Art. 9.2.a GDPR)

---

## 🔹 5. Third-Party Services Used

The App uses the following services:

### 🔸 Firebase Remote Config
To manage dynamic configuration of the App.

### 🔸 Firebase AdMob
To display personalized or non-personalized ads depending on the user’s settings.

### 🔸 Google Play Services / App Store Services
For essential platform functionalities.

📌 **None of these services receive menstrual cycle data**, as it is stored only locally.

---

## 🔹 6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users **over 13 years old**.  
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

We do not share personal data with third parties, except:

Menstrual cycle data **is never shared under any circumstances**.
